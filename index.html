      For Furniture & Mattress Businesses... LEARN MORE

      Welcome to the Sealy bedding wholesale website. You can now browse the various collections of items available for purchase from DOWNLITE including Sealy, Sealy Posturepedic, Sealy Posturepedic Optimum and Stearns & Foster.

      Once approved you will be able to see item prices on the website and be able to place your orders using a credit card or paypal. We can ship to your store, warehouse or even drop ship to your customers so you can keep your inventory levels minimized.

      If you have any questions regarding item selections please contact us Monday-Friday from 10 Am to 4 PM EST. If at anytime you need escalated help, please contact our Director of eCommerce Stefan Hunter - shunter@downlite.com.

      Sealy Bedding Wholesale

      Sealy has partnered with DOWNLITE to manufacture a line of bedding that includes pillows, mattress toppers, comforters, blankets, sheet sets and other bedding accessories filled with down and down alternative fillings.

      If you are a store looking to resell the bedding (mattress store, furniture store) or a commercial end user (interior designer, hotel, resort, B & B) please sign up for an account at the ‘start a business account’ link located above and to the right of the sliding images/banner.

      Please note this is a self serve platform and you will only be able to use a credit card or paypal for your purchases (please make sure to specify your Sealy Sales Rep at checkout).
